Firewall & WAF Strategy

The verdict on replacing the CSF and CMC components is clear: an integrated approach is superior.

Firewall (CSF) Replacement

Replacing CSF with the integrated, enhanced firewall of a premium suite is the logical path. This simplifies management, reduces complexity, and provides a unified support channel.

WAF Management (CMC) Replacement

A dedicated tool is unnecessary. Native cPanel/DirectAdmin tools and the superior, curated WAF rulesets within suites like cPGuard provide a far better solution for managing ModSecurity.
